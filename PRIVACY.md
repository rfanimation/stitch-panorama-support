# Privacy Policy for Stitch

**Last Updated: January 31, 2026**

## Overview

Stitch is designed with your privacy in mind. We believe your photos are yours alone.

## Data Collection

**We do not collect any data.**

- No analytics
- No crash reports sent to us
- No usage tracking
- No advertising

## Photo Processing

All image processing happens **entirely on your device**:

- Photos never leave your iPhone or iPad
- No images are uploaded to any server
- No cloud processing is used
- Panoramas are created locally using on-device AI

## Photo Library Access

Stitch requests access to your Photo Library to:

1. **Import photos** you select for stitching
2. **Save panoramas** back to your library

We only access photos you explicitly select. We cannot and do not browse your entire library.

## Purchases

In-app purchases are handled entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not have access to your payment information.

## Third-Party Services

Stitch does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

## Data Storage

- **Temporary files**: During processing, temporary files are created and automatically deleted
- **Preferences**: App settings are stored locally on your device
- **Purchase status**: Stored locally and verified through Apple's StoreKit

## Children's Privacy

Stitch does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

## Contact

Questions about this privacy policy? [Open an issue on GitHub](https://github.com/rfanimation/stitch-panorama/issues).
